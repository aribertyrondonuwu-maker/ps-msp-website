Program Profile

History & Accreditation

The Aquatic Resources Management program is one of the founding study programs since FPIK Unsrat was established in 1965. Its accreditation status has steadily improved — from rank B (Decree No. 2777/SK/BAN.PT/Akred/S/2016) to "Baik Sekali" / Very Good (Decree No. 12918/SK/BAN-PT/Ak-PPJ/S/XII/2021, valid through 25 November 2026) — and is now pursuing the "Unggul" / Excellent rating under LAM PTIP IAPS 1.0.

Vision & Mission

"To become an excellent Aquatic Resources Management program that is competent, of strong character, and acts with integrity in the sustainable management of aquatic resources — by 2029." Realized through three missions: quality education relevant to the world of work, research and community service for aquatic resource management, and character formation of graduates as agents of change.

OBE-MBKM Curriculum 2024

144–145 credit units (SKS) completed over 8 semesters, leading to the S.Pi. degree. This Outcome-Based Education & MBKM curriculum, last revised in 2024, maps 62 courses (55 compulsory + 7 elective) to 28 sub-learning outcomes (sub-CPL) and 7 graduate profiles: Researcher, Educator, Extension Officer, Manager, Entrepreneur, Aquaculture Consultant/Practitioner, and Employee/Civil Servant.

Tridharma Perguruan Tinggi (Three Pillars of Higher Education)

Research

76 research projects by permanent faculty (DTPS) worth IDR 5.04 billion over 2023–2025, funded from internal, national, and international sources (EDF, Rare Inc., FAO). 73.4% of funding came from external sources — evidence of DTPS competitiveness at national and global levels. 30 projects directly involved students.

Community Service

Permanent Faculty (DTPS)

16 permanent faculty members covering the full spectrum of aquatic resource management expertise — 3 full professors, 81.25% holding doctoral degrees from Indonesian and international universities.

Several DTPS also hold additional professional certifications: BKD Assessor, AMDAL A, EAFM, Halal Supervisor, and Professional Certification — beyond formal academic qualifications.
